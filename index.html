Hello

Hi, my name is Zac and I like to take photographs. You can see a selection of my work in the slider above. I'm currently studying at Plymouth University, which provides some of the most amazing backdrops for photographs that I've seen in the country. I look out for any opportunities to take pictures, and I try to have an eye for finding the perfect picture. I often travel back and forth between Plymouth and London, and therefore I often get the moment to take a large variation of photos with different backdrops and with different ideas.

Outside of photography, I also enjoy a rather nice Uni lifestyle, a combination of Netflix, reading and gaming. I try to keep an eye out for good cinematography too, as a lot of films, and even games now, provide some beautiful moments for millions of people. I'm currently working my way through Metal Gear Solid V, which has been a huge blast to go through.
